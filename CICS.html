The College of Informatics and Computing Sciences (CICS) Lipa offers BS in Information Technology program led by expert faculty, preparing graduates to design complex systems, develop innovative software, and apply computing technologies to meet diverse organizational needs.


Students select their specialization during their junior year, based on their interests and career goals.

Here are the three tracks available in the program:
Business Analytics (BA) - Focuses on the use of data analysis for informed business decisions
Network Technology (NT) - Covers designing and managing computer networks
Service Management (SM) - Emphasizes managing and improving IT services
The logos above are just the representation of the tracks designed by the officers of the organization, it is not affiliated nor the official logo of the department



The CICS department, represented by the Alpha Dragons, our is actively involved in various university and industry events. We excel in different activities and competitions, showcasing the talents and skills of our students. Just like a dragon's fiery spirit, we conquer challenges and soar to new heights.
