Who we are?

Tech Innovators Society [TechIS] is a community of third and fourth-year IT students, founded in 2023. As a recognized sub-organization, we're committed to empowering the next generation of tech leaders. We foster connections, build careers, and inspire innovation. By harnessing your technical skills, you can create impactful solutions both inside and outside our university. Join us and shape the future of the global knowledge economy.


Our Vision
This organization envisions itself as a community to be for men, women and for others, a community of developers that are passionate about uplifting communities and solving problems through science, technology, and innovation.

Our Mission
1. Empower people through technology and programming education; 2. Enlighten them to the power of innovation and problem-solving; 3. Nurture them to create meaningful technological solutions for the community; and 4. Use technology in helping communities.

At Tech Innovators Society, our objectives are multifaceted. We aim to be a student-led technology organization dedicated to fostering innovative solutions. Through collaborative efforts, we leverage technology to solve challenges and empower members to develop sustainable projects that address real-world issues.
